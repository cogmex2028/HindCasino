Detailed Payment Guides

💸 UPI Deposits

The default for Indian casinos. Instant deposits, free, INR-native. GPay, PhonePe, Paytm compared. Troubleshooting bank blocks.

🏦 Net Banking

IMPS / NEFT casino deposits. Slower than UPI for withdrawals but more reliable when UPI is blocked.

💳 AstroPay

India-friendly e-wallet that bridges your bank to casinos. Adds a small fee but bypasses some UPI restrictions.

📲 MuchBetter (coming soon)

UK-based e-wallet popular with sports bettors. Slightly slower than AstroPay but with broader merchant network.

₿ USDT, BTC, ETH

Stablecoin and major cryptos for casino play. Faster withdrawals, broader limits, but learning curve.

💳 Visa / Mastercard (coming soon)

Card deposits are instant but Indian-issued cards are increasingly blocked for gambling. Withdrawals are slow.

Which Payment Method Should You Use?

  • Default / most cases: UPI — free, fast, INR-native.
  • Fastest withdrawal: USDT (TRC-20) — under 30 minutes including network confirmation.
  • When UPI is blocked: Net banking → AstroPay → MuchBetter → crypto.
  • Higher anonymity: Bitcoin or USDT to a self-custody wallet.
  • If your bank blocks all gambling methods: crypto via a P2P exchange.
