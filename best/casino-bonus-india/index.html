When to Skip Welcome Bonuses Entirely

Welcome bonuses are not universally good. Skip them if:

  • You primarily play live dealer games (10% wagering contribution makes clearing painful).
  • You prefer larger bets per round (max-bet rule will trip you up).
  • You want flexibility to withdraw immediately after a winning session.
  • You don't enjoy slot play (the most wagering-friendly category).

Most experienced players know whether the bonus EV beats the flexibility cost. Newer players sometimes chase bonuses they're not positioned to clear — losing both the bonus and the original deposit. Choose accordingly.
