The defining category for Indian live casino. Tables stream from SuperSpade, Ezugi, and Evolution studios. Teen Patti runs in multiple variants (Classic, One Day, 20-20, Bonus); Andar Bahar in two or three formats. See our Teen Patti guide and Andar Bahar guide for rules and provider details.

Live Roulette

Evolution's Lightning Roulette is the standout live roulette in 2026 — random number multipliers on each spin pay outsized amounts when you hit a "lightning" number. Standard live European roulette runs at every casino in our listings.

Live Blackjack

Lowest house edge in the casino with basic strategy. Evolution's "Infinite Blackjack" allows unlimited players at one table. Speed Blackjack offers shorter rounds. All five operators in our listings publish Evolution blackjack.

Live Baccarat

Banker bet has ~1.06% house edge — the lowest table-game edge in the casino. Speed Baccarat (shorter rounds) and Dragon Tiger (a faster baccarat variant) are popular in India.
