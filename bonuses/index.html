Bonus Types Explained

1. Welcome Bonus (Match Deposit)

The standard offer. The casino matches your first deposit up to a cap. The numbers that matter: match percentage, cap, wagering requirement, and whether wagering applies to bonus only or bonus + deposit. Always pick bonus-only wagering. A 100% up to ₹20,000 with 30x bonus-only is better than a 200% up to ₹50,000 with 45x bonus + deposit.

2. Free Spins

Usually attached to a deposit bonus. Three things to check: which slot the spins are valid on (Sweet Bonanza, Big Bass Bonanza, and Book of Dead are typical), the value per spin (anything under ₹10 is throwaway), and the wagering on spin winnings (often higher than the cash bonus wagering).

3. No-Deposit Bonus

A small bonus or free spins given just for signing up, no deposit required. Rare in the Indian market because of high acquisition costs. When you see one, expect tight wagering (60x+) and a low max-cashout cap (often ₹2,000). Treat as try-before-you-buy, not a route to real winnings.

4. Cashback

Returns a percentage of your weekly losses. Cashback paid in real cash (no further wagering) is rare and valuable — Casino Days's Platinum VIP tier offers this. Cashback credited as bonus is more common and carries its own wagering.

5. Reload Bonus

Match bonus on subsequent deposits after the welcome. Usually 25–50% match with the same wagering pattern as the welcome bonus. Good ongoing value if you play regularly.

6. Tournament Prizes & Drops

Slot tournaments and Pragmatic Play Drops & Wins pay leaderboard prizes — typically real cash with no wagering. LuckySpin runs the most of these in our listings.

How to Calculate Playthrough Cost

Formula Playthrough cost = (Bonus × Wagering) ÷ (Bonus + Deposit)

Worked example: ₹20,000 bonus + ₹20,000 deposit + 30x wagering = (₹20,000 × 30) ÷ (₹20,000 + ₹20,000) = 15x your initial bankroll.

Below 15 is excellent. 15–20 is fair. Above 25 means you're working hard for marginal value.

This formula tells you how many times you need to play through your initial bankroll to clear the bonus — useful for comparing offers with different headline numbers and wagering rates.

Bonus Fine Print to Watch For

  • Bonus-only vs bonus + deposit wagering: always pick bonus-only.
  • Game contribution: slots usually 100%; live games often 10%; some games excluded entirely.
  • Max bet during wagering: typically ₹100–₹500. Exceeding the cap voids the bonus.
  • Bonus expiry: 7, 14, or 30 days are typical. Slot bonuses often shorter.
  • Max cashout cap: some no-deposit and free-spin bonuses cap winnings at ₹2,000–₹5,000.
  • Excluded countries / states: check the bonus is valid for your state.
  • Single-bonus-per-household rule: sharing an IP, device, or payment method usually disqualifies subsequent accounts.
