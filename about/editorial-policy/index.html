HindCasino Editorial Policy

This page sets out how HindCasino verifies casinos, sources information, fact-checks claims, corrects errors, and manages conflicts of interest. It applies to every listing, review, game guide, and informational article on the site.

Verification, Not Hands-On Review

HindCasino is a listing and verification platform — not a hands-on review site. We do not open casino accounts with player money, deposit funds, play through bonuses, or test withdrawals. We believe transparent verification from public sources delivers more value to readers than fabricated first-hand testing claims.

Every operator listing on HindCasino is based on publicly available information: regulator records, operator-published terms, game-provider certifications, and observable site behaviour. We disclose this framing on every listing page and never claim hands-on play we have not done.

An operator must score 8.0/10 or higher on the weighted average and at least 4.0/10 in every individual category to be listed. A low score in any single category is a disqualifier even if the overall average passes — for example, a casino with a great game library but unverifiable licensing will not be listed.

Information Sources

For each listing, we cross-reference at least three of the following sources:

  • The operator's own published terms, bonus terms, and licence pages
  • The licensing regulator's public register (Curaçao eGaming, MGA, UKGC, etc.)
  • Game provider certification listings (Evolution, Pragmatic Play, GLI, eCOGRA, iTech Labs)
  • Domain WHOIS and corporate registry records
  • Publicly available player-feedback aggregators (forums, dispute resolution services)

We do not source claims from operator marketing copy alone. If we cannot verify a claim from at least one independent source, we either qualify it ("the operator advertises…") or exclude it.

Fact-Checking

Every listing page is fact-checked by a second editor before publication. Numerical claims (bonus amounts, wagering multiples, deposit minimums, RTPs) must match the operator's currently published terms or an independently sourced figure. We re-check licensing and bonus terms monthly and update the "Reviewed" date on each listing.

For legal and tax content (such as the India legality guide), we cite statutes by section number and reference case law by case name and citation. We are not lawyers — legal content is presented as general information, not advice.

Corrections Policy

Errors happen. When we find an error — or one is reported to us — we update the page within 24 hours of confirmation and add a "Corrected on YYYY-MM-DD: [description]" note at the bottom of the affected section.

Report errors to editorial@hindcasino.com with the page URL and the specific claim you believe is wrong, ideally with a source we can verify. Substantive corrections are acknowledged within five business days.

Conflicts of Interest

HindCasino earns affiliate commission when a user registers at certain listed operators via tracked links. This relationship is disclosed on our Affiliate Disclosure page and on every page through the "Affiliate disclosure" badge in the hero section.

The following rules govern editorial independence:

  • Commission rates do not affect whether an operator appears in our listings.
  • Commission rates do not affect ranking or position within our listings.
  • Editorial staff are not compensated on the basis of commercial performance of pages they write.
  • We refuse "guaranteed top placement" commercial proposals.
  • Authors and reviewers declare any direct or indirect financial interest in a listed operator. If an interest exists, that author does not work on that operator's listing.
  • If an operator pulls its affiliate programme but still meets our criteria, we continue listing them.
  • If an operator's licensing or audited-provider status changes, we de-list them regardless of commission.

Author Identification

Every article on HindCasino is attributed to the HindCasino Editorial Team and shows a "Reviewed on" date. Where individual editors take responsibility for specific guides (such as the legality guide), we add their name and area of expertise to the byline.

Responsible Gambling Position

We include responsible-gambling messaging on every page that promotes a casino operator. We do not target readers under 18, do not display offers to logged-out users in restricted Indian states, and link to helplines in our footer.

Updates to This Policy

We review this editorial policy annually and update it when our processes change. Material changes are noted with a "Policy updated YYYY-MM-DD" entry at the top of the page.
