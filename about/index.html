About HindCasino

HindCasino is an independent listing and verification platform for online casinos that serve Indian players. We exist for a single purpose: to help Indian players quickly identify which online casinos are properly licensed, use audited game providers, and support Indian payment methods — so the decision about where to play (or whether to play at all) is informed, not blind.

What HindCasino Is

HindCasino is a directory that lists online casinos meeting a set of public, verifiable criteria. We check each operator's licensing record, audited game provider list, payment support for India, published bonus terms, and responsible-gambling tooling. If an operator meets our criteria, we list it. If it does not, we don't.

The platform is run by an independent editorial team based in India. We hold no ownership stake in any casino we list. Revenue comes from affiliate commissions paid by some listed operators when a user registers from our site — disclosed transparently on our Affiliate Disclosure page. Commission rates do not affect listing decisions or rankings.

What HindCasino Is Not

Three important clarifications about what we do not do:

  • We do not run hands-on reviews with player accounts. We do not deposit our own money, play through bonuses, or test withdrawal times. Reviews on competitor sites that claim "we tested 50 casinos with our own money" are typically marketing copy — we believe transparent verification beats fabricated first-hand testing.
  • We do not operate or own any casino. Every casino on our site is a third-party operator with its own licence, support team, and dispute process. If you have an issue with an operator, contact their customer support directly — we cannot resolve account or payment issues on the operator's behalf.
  • We do not guarantee outcomes, winnings, or experiences. Listing on HindCasino is not an endorsement. The decision to register and play at any casino is entirely the player's own.

Our Listing Methodology

Every operator listed on HindCasino is verified against the following public criteria. The full scoring framework is published on our Editorial Policy page.

  1. Licensing. Curaçao, Malta, UKGC, Kahnawake, or Anjouan licence with public regulator record.
  2. Game providers. Operator must list only RNG-audited providers (Evolution, Pragmatic Play, NetEnt, Play'n GO, Hacksaw, etc.) — independently certified by eCOGRA, GLI, or iTech Labs.
  3. Payments for India. UPI must be officially supported. Net banking, AstroPay, or crypto must be listed as alternatives. Published withdrawal windows must be reasonable (under 72h for any method).
  4. Bonus transparency. Wagering requirements, max-bet rules, and game contribution percentages must be clearly published. We don't list operators that bury essential terms.
  5. Responsible gambling. Deposit limits, session timers, cooling-off, and self-exclusion must be publicly available.
  6. KYC and AML. Operator must publish KYC procedures consistent with its licensing framework.

A casino must score 8.0/10 or higher on the weighted average, and at least 4.0/10 in every category, to appear in our listings. We re-verify licensing and provider records monthly.

Why We Exist

Indian players searching for online casinos face a difficult information environment. Social media is full of operator ads that don't disclose licensing or terms. SEO-driven "review" sites often invent first-hand testing claims to build false trust. Many operators that appear on the first page of search results don't even hold a verifiable licence.

HindCasino's role is narrow but useful: provide a transparent, criteria-based listing that lets Indian players quickly distinguish properly licensed operators from cowboys. We provide the information; the decision about where to play remains with the player.

Editorial Independence

We accept affiliate revenue from some listed operators. Commission rates vary by operator and are not disclosed individually because they're commercially sensitive. However, the following rules govern our editorial process:

  • Commission rates do not affect whether an operator is listed.
  • Commission rates do not affect ranking or position within listings.
  • If an operator stops paying commission, we continue listing them if they still meet our criteria.
  • If an operator's licensing or audited-provider status changes, we de-list them regardless of commission.
  • We refuse "guaranteed top placement" commercial proposals. Rankings are based on verification scoring, not payment.

Responsible Gambling

Online gambling is for entertainment. It is not income, not a side hustle, not a way to recover financial difficulty. Every casino we list is required to publish deposit limits, self-exclusion, and session timers. Set them before you play.

If you or someone you know needs support, free help is available from Connecting Trust (helpline +91 9920076445), BeGambleAware, or GamCare. We do not provide gambling addiction services ourselves — but we link to those who do, in our footer on every page.
