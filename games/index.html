Live Indian Games

🎴 Teen Patti

India's most-played card game. Rules, hand rankings (Trail → Pure Sequence → Sequence → Colour → Pair → High Card), variants (Classic, One Day, Teen Patti 20-20, Bonus, SuperSpade), RTP ~96.5–97.7%.

🃏 Andar Bahar

The fastest card game in any live casino lobby. Inside vs outside, 30-second rounds, ~2.15% house edge on main bet — better than most table games.

✈️ Aviator & Crash

The breakout game of 2023–2026. Cash out before the plane flies away. Variants: JetX, Cash Show, Plinko.

♠️ Live Blackjack

Lowest house edge in the casino (0.5% with basic strategy). Live Evolution and Pragmatic Live tables.

🎯 Roulette

European (single-zero, 2.7% edge) vs American (double-zero, 5.26% edge). Live Lightning Roulette by Evolution.

🎰 Baccarat

Banker bet has ~1.06% edge — second-lowest in the casino. Speed and Dragon Tiger variants.

🃏 Rummy

Classified as a game of skill under Indian law. Different legal treatment from chance games.

🎮 Live Game Shows

Crazy Time, Monopoly Live, Funky Time, Lightning Storm — Evolution's high-production game-show category.

🐉 Dragon Tiger

Simplest live table game in the casino — one card per side, ~3.73% house edge, 25-second rounds.

🎲 Sic Bo

Ancient Chinese three-dice game. 2.78% house edge on Big/Small bets — competitive with European Roulette.

🎯 Plinko

Crypto-native ball-drop game. Provably-fair versions at 99% RTP — among the highest available.
