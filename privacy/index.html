Only collected if you interact with us — for example, by emailing us or submitting our contact form:

See the dedicated cookies section below.

What We Do Not Collect

HindCasino does not require any account registration. We do not collect financial information, deposit amounts, casino account details, gambling history, identity documents, or KYC data. Any such data you provide to a casino operator listed on our site is between you and that operator — not us.

We process the data above only for the following legitimate purposes:

  • Affiliate attribution: recording that a click from our site led to a registration at a listed operator, so we are correctly credited.

Your Rights Under the DPDP Act 2023

If you are in India, the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 gives you the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to correction: request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure: request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to withdraw consent: stop our processing where you previously consented.
  • Right to grievance redress: contact our Data Protection Officer to raise concerns.
  • Right to nominate: nominate another person to exercise rights on your behalf in case of incapacity.

Exercise any of these rights by emailing privacy@hindcasino.com from the email address associated with your records (if any). We respond to substantive requests within thirty days as required by the Act.

Data Retention

  • Server logs: 30 days, then anonymised or deleted.
  • Analytics data: aggregated only after 24 hours, retained at the aggregate level only.
  • Affiliate tracking cookies: 30–90 days depending on operator.
  • Email correspondence: retained for two years for support history, then deleted unless you ask earlier.

Children

HindCasino is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us at privacy@hindcasino.com and we will delete it.

Security

We use HTTPS across the entire site, restrict administrative access to our team, and follow standard security practices for the type of data we handle. No system is perfectly secure; we cannot guarantee absolute protection of data in transit or storage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Material changes are noted with a "Policy updated YYYY-MM-DD" entry at the top. Your continued use of the site after a change indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
